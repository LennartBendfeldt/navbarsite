Lennart's Charlotte Restaurant WIN-ners

This site is for my top five favorite Charlotte restaurants!

Top 5

  • Pinky's Westside Grill

    Pinky's Westside grill (twice featured on diners, drive-ins, and dives) is a casual bar and restaurant located in Charlotte slinging burgers, dogs, and beer.

  • 131 Main

    131 Main delivers fresh, upscale food with a near-sultry atmosphere and extensive wine list.

  • Machu Picchu

    Contrasting with it's outward appearance, Machu Picchu delivers comfortable and delivious Peruvian food in South Charlotte's Pineville neighborhood.

  • Waldhorn German Restaurant

    An authentically German-owned restaurant located just minutes from another on my list.

  • Haymaker

    Upscale class in an uptown highrise. Located across from the BB&T Ballpark.
